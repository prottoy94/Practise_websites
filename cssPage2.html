Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a free-to-play open-world action role-playing game developed and published by Kuro Games, released globally on May 23, 2024 for Android, iOS, and Windows, with later releases for PlayStation 5 (January 2025) and macOS (March 2025).

The game immerses players in a post-apocalyptic world that blends remnants of ancient civilizations with futuristic elements. Initially inspired by Death Stranding, its world-building focuses on themes of reconnection and rediscovery. Its combat mechanics, influenced by Punishing: Gray Raven, offer fast-paced, skill-intensive battles, while its "Echo system"—which allows players to harness powers from defeated enemies—draws parallels with the Pokémon series.

Though often compared to Genshin Impact for its open-world format and art style, Wuthering Waves sets itself apart with a deeper emphasis on combat fluidity and gameplay complexity.

Wuthering Waves is a 2024 free-to-play open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games, set in a post-apocalyptic world called Solaris-3, where players control an amnesiac protagonist named Rover as they explore the aftermath of a cataclysmic event known as the Lament. Combining fast-paced, skill-based combat inspired by Punishing: Gray Raven and an “Echo system” similar to Pokémon, the game allows players to absorb abilities from defeated enemies to customize their playstyle. The traversal system is highly fluid, featuring wall-running, air dashing, and grappling without stamina limitations outside combat. With a focus on combat depth over traditional gacha formulas, Wuthering Waves introduces unique Resonators (characters) each with their own Intro and Outro skills, alongside strategic team-building through a pity-based gacha system. Updates like version 2.4 and the massive 2.6 patch continue to expand the story, characters, and endgame content such as Tower of Adversity and roguelike Depths of Illusive Realm. Initially released for PC and mobile on May 23, 2024, with PlayStation 5 and macOS versions following in early 2025, the game has been praised for its exhilarating combat and mobility, although some criticism remains regarding polish compared to genre giants like Genshin Impact


As of Version 2.6, released on August 28, 2025, Wuthering Waves introduces a new explorable region called Sanguis Plateaus, expanding the game's world south of Septimont and pushing forward the main story with two major chapters: By Sun’s Burning Hand and By Moon’s Fated Light. The update adds two powerful 5-star Resonators: Augusta, an Electro broadblade wielder, and Iuno, whose element remains undisclosed but is expected to debut in Phase II. Their signature weapons—Thunderflare Dominion and Moongazer’s Sigil—enhance their synergy and build flexibility. Players can also collect new Echoes like The False Sovereign, Lady of the Sea, and Corrosaurus, enriching the game's summonable and passive ability options. Additionally, rerun banners for popular characters like Carlotta and The Shorekeeper are featured, and the update includes various bug fixes and performance enhancements, making this the game's largest and most ambitious patch to date.

How to play the game

To play Wuthering Waves, you begin as the Rover, an amnesiac protagonist awakening in a post-apocalyptic world filled with mysterious threats and remnants of old civilizations. Your objective is to explore the vast open world of Solaris-3, completing story quests, defeating powerful enemies known as Tacet Discords, and recruiting characters called Resonators through a gacha system. The game features fluid traversal mechanics like wall-running, gliding, and grappling, alongside dynamic, fast-paced combat requiring strategic dodging, parrying, and skill combos. Players can customize their playstyle by collecting and equipping Echoes—creature-based powers absorbed from enemies. Progressing through the story unlocks new regions, challenges, and powerful gear to strengthen your team.

Current Events

As part of the ongoing Version 2.6 update, players can participate in limited-time events tied to the new region Sanguis Plateaus. The featured event, "Sunfire Trials," offers combat challenges that reward upgrade materials, Echoes, and premium currency. There are also daily login bonuses, time-limited boss raids, and the “Rift Harmonization” event, which lets players test new characters like Augusta and Iuno in trial battles for extra rewards. Additionally, rerun banners for fan-favorite Resonators and themed cosmetics are available for a limited time. Players are encouraged to complete these events before the update ends to maximize their resources and unlock exclusive content.
